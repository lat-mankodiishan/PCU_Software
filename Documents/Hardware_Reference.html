5. Watchdog

  • IWDG: independent watchdog on LSI (~32 kHz). Prescaler = 8 → counter = 4 kHz; Reload = 4095 → ≈ 1.0 s timeout.
  • Refreshed by supervisor_task every 200 ms via watchdog_refresh() → 5× margin.
  • __HAL_DBGMCU_FREEZE_IWDG() is commented out in main.c — IWDG keeps counting while the debugger is paused. Be aware during JTAG single-step.
